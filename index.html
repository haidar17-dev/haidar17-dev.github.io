MY FAVORITE BOOKS

Hari Gini
Masih Galau?!

Writer : Hilmy Mubarok

In this book, I learned some life lessons that helped me become a better person. It taught me to always have faith and accept whatever fate Allah has in store for me, and to always be content with whatever Allah has in store for me.

Atomic Habits


Writer : James Clear

“Atomic Habits” is a book that teaches me to always establish a system for practicing even the smallest habits every day to achieve your desired results. For example, if the goal is to become a runner, then the system involves going for a jog every day, even if it's just for 10 minutes. And more one important point: a plan will never be existed without “action.”

Dopamine Nation


Writer : Anna Lembke

From this book, I learned that the reason people struggle to be productive and consistent in one area is because we have habits that release large amounts of dopamine—such as scrolling through social media and playing video games—commonly referred to as “instant dopamine.” This is also closely tied to how we cultivate small habits to become the person or achieve the identity we aspire to.
